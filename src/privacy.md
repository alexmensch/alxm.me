## What personal data we collect

### Counselling clients

When you enquire about or engage in counselling services, we may collect:

- Your name, email address, and phone number (for booking and communication)
- Appointment scheduling information (processed by Calendly)
- Calendar invites containing your name and session time (sent via Google Calendar)
- Payment and invoicing details (processed by Stripe and Xero)
- Video session connection data (processed by Zoom)

We do not store digital clinical or therapeutic session notes. No records of session content are held electronically.

The legal basis for processing this data is **contractual necessity** (to provide the counselling service you have requested) and **legitimate interest** (to communicate with you about your enquiry).

### Website visitors

This website does not use analytics, tracking scripts, or any form of behavioural monitoring. We do not collect personal data from visitors browsing this site.

Our hosting provider, Cloudflare, may process minimal technical data (such as IP addresses) in server logs for security and performance purposes. This processing is carried out under Cloudflare's own privacy policy on the basis of **legitimate interest**.

## Cookies and tracking

This website does not set any cookies and does not use any tracking technology. There are no first-party cookies, third-party cookies, or analytics scripts of any kind.

## Data retention

- **Financial records** are retained for six years in accordance with HMRC requirements.
- **Email correspondence** is retained for the duration of the therapeutic relationship and a reasonable period afterwards to handle any follow-up enquiries.
- **Appointment data** is retained by Calendly in accordance with their own retention policies.
- **No digital clinical records** are maintained. Therapeutic session content is not recorded or stored electronically.

## Your rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erase** your personal data (in certain circumstances)
- **Restrict** the processing of your data
- **Data portability** --- receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
- **Object** to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [hello@alxm.me](mailto:hello@alxm.me).

If you are not satisfied with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the [Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)](https://ico.org.uk).
